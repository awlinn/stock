History of Na'Vi

Natus Vincere (Na'Vi) is a professional esports organization founded in 2009. They initially gained popularity in the Counter-Strike 1.6 scene, where they achieved numerous victories and became one of the most successful teams in the game's history. Since then, Na'Vi has expanded to other esports titles, including Dota 2 and CS:GO, and continues to be a prominent figure in the competitive gaming scene.

Country of Origin

Na'Vi was founded in Ukraine.
